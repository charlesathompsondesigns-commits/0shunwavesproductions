About

Angelina Beamon

CEO & Founder  ·  "Angie"

"Putting me and my people on — one wave at a time."

I'm Angelina Beamon, best known as "Angie" — the CEO of Oshun Waves Production. I'm a Black creative, dancer, and current college graduate with the dreams of putting me and my people on.

Throughout my journey it first started with me building community at my college by becoming the President of the Black Student Union as well as creating the organization. Through the help and collaboration of my partners Marilyn Carroll and Kaiya Brooks we worked hard to create a safe space for cultural expression, creativity, and support — which has furthered my love for community and artivism.

Oshun Waves Production will further my passion and love for creating by continuing to curate those same spaces on a much larger scale than ever seen before.

Dancer Creative Director Community Builder Artivist BSU Founder
