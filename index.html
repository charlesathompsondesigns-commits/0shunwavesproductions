Black Art

Oshun Waves Production is a Black-led creative production company that curates immersive fashion shows and editorial experiences rooted in artivism, cultural identity, and the belief that Black art is resistance.

Projects

Brand New Day

Brand New Day united Black Student Union leaders at Daley College, fusing art, activism, and Black culture through fashion.

Roots to Reverence

Roots to Reverence bridges multicultural spaces through Black expression, exploring themes of Southern Gothic, Western Couture, and Icy Opulence.
